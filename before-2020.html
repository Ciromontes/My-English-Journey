Things I Was Doing Before

A reflection on my life before March 2020 and how everything changed

May 11, 2025

Life Before the Pandemic

Hello everyone. Before March 2020, life looked very different for all of us. Today I'd like to take you on a journey through my life before everything changed, from daily routines to unexpected challenges. This is my story.

First News of COVID-19

Before March 2020, I remember hearing the first news about COVID-19. Around December 2019, I was watching the news when they reported a virus in China. I never imagined it would spread so much and have such an unhook impact on the world.

At that time, I was taking driving lessons because a friend who owned a cheese business needed a driver to help distribute his products.

Religious Activities

As one of Jehovah's Witnesses, I was also visiting a small town called Adios, sharing the good news of the Bible. On the picture you can see the exact day the lockdown was announced. We were distributing invitations to commemorate the death of Jesus.

Regarding my studies, I was teaching in-person Bible courses, but I was not enrolled in any secular studies. I was manufacturing and selling cleaning products, and I was working from home.

Recreational Activities

When it came to sports, I joined a couple of times a week. I also enjoyed cycling and hiking in the mountains. I spent my time with friends and my mother.

During the Pandemic

During the pandemic, we found new ways to stay connected. We met only with friends for recreational activities, attending via Zoom, playing games, and participating in Bible-based encouragement conferences.

I also attended international conventions, one of which was "Always Rejoice" - a beautiful event that strengthened our faith and hope during those difficult times. We took care of others and gave each other support. Despite the challenges, we always felt Jehovah's guidance and his fatherly care.
